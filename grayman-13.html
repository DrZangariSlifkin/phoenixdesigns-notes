Court and Zoya are together, then accidentally stumble into the midst of a string of murders of people in the artificial intelligence community. They try to mostly stay out of it while continuing to get involved. Zack Hightower ends up working security for a man (Anton Hinton) who is a billionaire AI genius because he is afraid he will be targeted. Bunch of stuff happens, Court helps take down the bad guy, who is an AI created by Anton Hinton. In the end Zoya is captured by the Chinese and being traded to Russia, Zack kills Anton and is arrested, Court is promised the help of the CIA by Jim Pace. Court goes to Matt Hanley for help in the epilogue.
